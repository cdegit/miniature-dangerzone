Our Story

Bezal is a local, family-owned business that has been serving Vancouver's designer furniture needs since 1993. Bezal was founded by Linda and Jeff Peterson with the intent of supplying Vancouverites with the best furniture in the world.

  • Linda Peterson

    Linda studied Industrial Design at Langara University where she fell in love with the art of crafting furniture. In 1993, after years of honing her skills, she founded Bezal. Now, she can always be found working in the studio dreaming up new creations.

  • Jeff Peterson

    Jeff met Linda shortly before she founded Bezal in 1993. He had just entered the world of high-end furniture with no previous experience. However, he had the business skills to get Bezal running and making money. They were a perfect match. Jeff is now busy organizing Bezal's many community events.
