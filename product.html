Sargon

Our French-inspired take on the traditional sofa redefines for the current age. It hugs low to the floor, is deep in profile, sleek and streamlined, and has a both casual and sophisticated appeal. It is crafted with solid lightweight aluminum frame, hand-stitched with the highest quality genuine leather, and delicately pieced together at our very own local workshops. Sargon is upholstered in one of 3 classic leatherettes: Wine red, Charcoal Grey, or Creamy Beige. This modern yet minimalistic piece is versatile in any room. As the standout centerpiece or as a complementary furnishing, Sargon will add flavor to your space.
